Zevism: Instructions for New People

If you are new to the Temple of Zeus, we encourage you to read everything on this website. Some of the articles may be outdated, as our websites are currently undergoing major updates. However, everything you find here will make total sense.

Membership in the Temple of Zeus is open and free. Thanks to donations, we are able to make knowledge openly and freely available while we continue our research. We keep everyone updated as new knowledge and material become available.

We encourage you to join our forums. Feel free to ask questions in any of the groups, but please read and study the material here, as many questions can be answered through personal study. We are a knowledge and study-based community.

Once you have studied this website, and if you are serious about making a commitment to Zeus and the Gods, this will be your initiation into Zevism.

After performing your commitment ritual, you should begin a power meditation program. Power meditation is the foundation of Zevism. You will find support in the forums for any questions you may have about meditation experiences and related topics.
