Frequently Asked Questions About the Gods and Daemons

updated by: High Priest Zevios Metathronos

Common questions answered: from the nature of the Gods to practical methods of communion.

What are the "Gods of Hell"?

The "Gods of Hell" are the ancient deities of humanity, originally worshipped in the Pagan world. They are the entities that the ancient priests communed with, seeking knowledge, power, and divine favor. These practices were normal and accepted in the ancient world but were later demonized by Abrahamic religions.

Why were these practices demonized?

With the advent of Christianity, Islam, and Judaism, the ancient Gods were recast as demons, and their worshippers were labeled as heretics and sorcerers. This was a deliberate effort to suppress and vilify the ancient wisdom and establish the dominance of monotheistic faiths.

Is the knowledge of summoning these entities valid?

Yes, much of this knowledge is accurate and was used by ancient priesthoods on a regular basis. It was only after the advent of Abrahamic religions that these practices were deemed "evil." The knowledge persists today, hidden in grimoires and secret societies.

Who can communicate with these entities?

Communication with these entities is not for the faint of heart or the casually curious. It is a path reserved for the spiritually evolved, the adepts, and those who are willing to put in the effort and undergo the necessary initiations.

How does one communicate with their Guardian Daemon?

Communication with your Guardian Daemon involves a hierarchy of stages, from recognizing omens and feeling their presence to receiving direct advice in dreams and internalizing their guidance. See Communication with Your Guardian Daemon.

What are omens, and how do they relate to communication?

Omens are signs, numbers, or other things that "come to your attention" and are orchestrated by your Guardian Daemon to guide you. Recognizing and interpreting these omens is the first stage of communication.

Can I feel the presence of my Guardian Daemon?

Yes. As your awareness deepens, you can feel the presence of your Guardian Daemon. This is a subtle sensation that confirms their protective oversight and begins to build trust and attunement.

How do feelings and intuitions play a role?

Your Guardian Daemon can communicate through feelings and intuitions, instilling in you an "internal compass" that guides your actions and decisions, helping you grow in power and live a blessed life.

Can I see my Guardian Daemon in dreams?

At advanced levels, yes. Your Guardian Daemon can appear to you in dreams, where they give direct advice, reveal hidden knowledge, or offer profound insights.

What is the highest level of communication?

The highest level is when you have internalized the path of the Gods, knowing in advance what you should be doing. Your Guardian Daemon then teaches you the next levels of advancement.

Can the Gods intervene directly in my life?

Yes, in certain cases, the Gods may intervene directly to save you from decisions of very negative consequences or to promote specific opportunities. This is reserved for those deeply committed to the path of Zevism.

What can deter me from communicating with my Guardian Daemon?

Neglect of meditation, failure to actively engage with the Gods, negative or detrimental behavior, psychological blocks, and excessive focus on materiality can all hinder communication. See What Connects and Disconnects You.

How does meditation help?

Meditation opens the channels of communication, allowing you to perceive the signs, feelings, and intuitions that constitute the divine dialogue. Consistent, powerful meditation strengthens this connection.

Why is active engagement with the Gods important?

Active engagement through ritual, prayer, and conscious communication enhances the bond with your Guardian Daemon, creating a reciprocal relationship that deepens over time.

How does positive behavior affect communication?

Positive actions create a clear, unobstructed channel for divine communication, allowing you to perceive and act upon the guidance being offered. The Gods reward effort and intent.

What role do psychological integration and inner harmony play?

A mind free from psychological blocks and internal conflicts is open to divine communication. Inner harmony allows you to perceive the signs and feelings that constitute your Guardian Daemon's guidance.

Why is spiritual focus important?

A life focused on spiritual evolution and divine priorities creates a strong, clear connection to the Gods. This spiritual focus attracts the attention of the divine, forging a powerful bond with your Guardian Daemon.

What is the significance of the ancient priesthoods' practices?

The ancient priesthoods' practices were sacred rituals performed by spiritually evolved individuals. They were normal and accepted in the ancient world but were later demonized by Abrahamic religions. These practices form the foundation of Zevist praxis today.
