To do this, it is very important to have open, active, and powerful chakras, especially in your hands and feet. For more information, click on the links below:

Now, I speak from experience here. If you have a virus in you that you haven’t even experienced the symptoms of yet, the drain from healing another, or even putting an aura of protection upon another will make it manifest. Worse yet, if you are even the slightest bit ill, never perform any healing or other energy workings upon another. Experience has shown that the soul of the loved one will connect and take even more energy than intended, especially in a desperate situation. In order to effectively heal a loved one, you must be healthy yourself. This includes, as I mentioned in the above, running energy through you from an external source. Also note that if you have any minor ailment, giving healing energy to another can worsen that ailment. Please be aware of this.

8.  As with healing the self, severe cases may require several healing sessions per day. This also depends upon your own personal powers. The more powerful you are, the more powerful and effective your healing energy will be.

## Healing Others: Key Points

**Distance doesn't matter.** Healing energy can be directed across any distance. Visualize the person clearly, see the affected area, and direct energy into it.

**EVOKE, don't invoke.** When healing others, direct the energy outward through your hands or aura. Do NOT absorb the illness into yourself. This is the most common mistake.

**Multiple sessions are usually needed.** Chronic conditions require sustained daily work. Don't expect one session to cure everything.
