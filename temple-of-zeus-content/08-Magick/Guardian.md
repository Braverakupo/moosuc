# How to Find Your Guardian Daemon

**High Priest Zevios Metathronos**

Practical methods for discovering who your Guardian Daemon is and establishing first contact.

## Methods of Discovery

There are several methods for contacting and identifying your Guardian Daemon. The most important thing is patience and genuine intent. Your Guardian Daemon wants to make contact with you. They will help you with spirituality and how to better relate to Father Zeus, especially when you are new. They will also help with meditation and other spiritual exercises.

### Method 1: The Ouija Board

One effective method is to use a [Ouija Board](Ouija.php). You don't need another person working the board with you. Working the board alone is often preferable. When there are two or more people working a Ouija Board, this can create distractions and cause the board to give inaccurate answers. There are also many times when one wishes to ask personal or private questions. This is best done alone, where one can relax into a trance, which is also helpful in operating a Ouija Board.

Ask directly: "Is there a Guardian Daemon for me?" or "What is the name of my Guardian Daemon?" When new, the Daemon may first communicate through letters and abbreviations: U R (you are), C U (see you) for goodbye. This is how you know the contact is real: if you expected full words but receive abbreviations, you're dealing with an intelligence outside yourself.

### Method 2: The Pendulum

Another method for finding out who your Guardian Daemon is, is to use a [pendulum](Pendulum.php). You will need a decent pendulum and a wide-mouthed glass jar.

Start out by asking how many letters are in his or her name. The pendulum will start to circle, then stop, then circle again, then stop and again make circles until it completely stops. Count the number of times it makes circles, and here you will have the number of letters in your Daemon's name.

Dangle the pendulum, holding it between your thumb and index finger in the glass jar, keeping your hand outside of and directly over the jar. Begin with the first letter and ask the pendulum if it is A, B, C, and so on. When the pendulum hits the side of the glass jar, write down the letter and continue on to the next letter in his or her name, again going through the alphabet.

Make sure you are completely relaxed and preferably in a trance for an accurate reading.

### Method 3: Telepathic Communication

Those who are experienced normally communicate with their Daemons telepathically. This develops naturally with consistent meditation practice over time. The Guardian Daemon will make themselves known through recurring thoughts, impressions, and intuitive guidance that grows clearer as the practitioner advances.

## Signs of Connection

An interesting confirmation that sometimes occurs is finding that your Guardian Daemon rules an astrological point that is exactly on a significant position in your natal chart. This isn't always the case, but when it happens, it's a powerful confirmation of a relationship that often spans past lives, sometimes thousands of years.

Practitioners have reported their Guardian Daemon appearing to them in dreams after successful identification. Others have found that after discovering their Guardian's identity, a series of synchronicities and improvements in their life immediately follow. The relationship, once established, becomes one of the most rewarding experiences in the entire path.

> For those of you who are doing yoga and/or chakra work, just be persistent. It can take a couple of months when starting out. You may be too young or inexperienced to understand what the energy work is really all about at first, but you will definitely feel the energy. With consistent practice, the connection to your Guardian Daemon deepens naturally.

Our Guardian Daemons can help us in our magick and spirituality. They are with us for the long term, guiding our development across this lifetime and beyond.
