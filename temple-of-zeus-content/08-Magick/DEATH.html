Death Spell

WARNING: This working is permitted only as spiritual self-defense against documented physical life-threats, or in cases of justified self-judgment, per Zevist ethics. Read the full ethical framework before any application: Life Ethics — Death & Slaying Ethics. Misuse violates Ma'at and incurs heavy weight upon the practitioner.

If you have access to any personal article of the offender, this will help.

  1. Construct an effigy or a poppit, using the article.
  2. Go to the cemetery and get some dirt.
  3. You need to focus on the poppit, visualizing the offender, all of the justified anger you possibly can, especially during its construction. It is imperative here to stay focused with intent. Try to do this during a waning Moon. A good time is when the Moon is in Scorpio or Capricorn.
  4. Construct a small box. Cardboard is fine.
  5. Light a black candle and concentrate intensely upon the spiritual judgment of the enemy. DO NOT BE DISTURBED OR LOSE YOUR FOCUS!
  6. Put the effigy into its box.
  7. Bury it in dirt inside the box.
  8. Save some of the dirt.
  9. Take it outside or back to the graveyard and bury it, placing the reserved dirt on top of the box, *knowing* spiritual judgment will be rendered.
  10. Do not think about the spell or the enemy, as this will interfere with its working.

To Inflict Spiritual Harm using the aura:

INFLICTING SPIRITUAL HARM (Self-Defense / Justified Judgment Only):

A poppit here is not necessary, nor is any ceremonial magick, but you must be much more powerful than the intended target. This is best done while the offending party is asleep. Put a grey aura around the person.

Affirm: "The grey destructive energy is inflicting spiritual harm upon [name of person]."

This work is best begun on a Saturday or a Tuesday during a waning Moon. Be sure to clean your aura and chakras following each working.

This can be done following the above ritual with the poppit and repeated every night during the cycle of the waning Moon until spiritual judgment is rendered.
