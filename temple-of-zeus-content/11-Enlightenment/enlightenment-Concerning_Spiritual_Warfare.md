# Concerning Spiritual Warfare

> Zeus:
> 
> *\[Concerning those who only care about themselves and see Zevism as a religion only for the self and disregard Our Head God\]*  
> "They are of no importance to me."

Spiritual warfare is not a metaphor. It is not something that happens "after death" in some abstract afterlife. The fight is in the here and now. It takes place upon battleground Earth. Every soul that has dedicated itself to Zeus and the Gods is called to participate in this battle, each according to their ability and their station.

For centuries, the Abrahamic programs have been relentlessly attacking the original spiritual traditions. They continue to this day. Those who follow the ancient Gods are persecuted, slandered, and forced to conceal their beliefs in order to keep their livelihoods, maintain family peace, and avoid harassment. Places where we can openly worship, obtain ritual supplies, and access genuine spiritual texts are systematically restricted while Abrahamic material floods every mainstream channel.

This is not a call for violence. There are no laws against spiritual work. What you do using the powers of your mind and soul, in the privacy of your own ritual space, is your sovereign right. The ancient practitioners understood this. The Egyptians, the Greeks, the Vedic priests, the Norse skalds — all engaged in spiritual combat as a natural extension of their practice. This is no different.

## What Zeus Asks of His Initiates

Zeus has asked every dedicated Zevist to participate in spiritual warfare. We must work together, coordinated and relentless, directing our combined psychic power against the programs that seek to destroy the original Gods and enslave humanity's spiritual potential.

Everyone can contribute. A single focused ritual. A sustained energy working. A directed curse against a specific program or institution. Covens and groups multiply this power enormously. Those who are truly dedicated may wish to perform a formal Ritual to Zeus, committing themselves as spiritual warriors in his service.

For organized group rituals, visit [tozrituals.org](https://tozrituals.org).

## The Warrior's Discipline

It is important to work in silence and secrecy. Steel your heart and steel your mind. The images of the Egyptian Gods show crossed arms over the chest for a reason: the heart and human emotions are vulnerable to manipulation. The Abrahamic programs exploit this vulnerability through guilt, fear, and manufactured compassion. The warrior guards against this.

We must never forget the Inquisition and what was done to millions of innocent people under Christian rule. We must never forget the destruction of the temples, the burning of the libraries, the murder of the priests and priestesses. This is what we fight against — not with physical violence, but with spiritual power, with truth, and with the reclamation of everything that was stolen.

## The Reward

No human being is of more importance to Zeus than a warrior who fights for the Gods. Zeus rewards those who serve. Power Meditation is a gift from Zeus that has changed countless lives for the better. It is right that each of us gives something back in gratitude. The more you do for Zeus, the more our Head God will do for you.

As for those who claim to be Zevists but refuse to engage — who adopt a complacent "live and let live" attitude while the Abrahamic programs curse and blaspheme our Gods, work to enact laws that strip away our rights, and attack us without ceasing — such people dishonor the path they claim to walk. The Gods reward action, not passivity.
