# Chakra Focus Meditation

author: **High Priest Zevios Metathronos**

This foundational Base Chakra meditation is the universal template to be applied systematically to all seven main chakras along the spine.

The meditation below is to be adjusted progressively to all the chakras, starting from the Base Chakra and going up to the Crown.

You must build the levels upward until mastery exists in Level 4 for all the seven main chakras.

Levels 1 and 2 should be mastered for all chakras, all the way up to Level 4 on the Crown Chakra.

**This is a cornerstone of meditation.**

By practicing this multi-sensory focus on each energy center in turn, you activate awareness of them, moving them from abstract concepts into tangible, felt realities within your own body. This initial activation is the crucial first step.

On higher levels, this sustained, focused attention does not merely observe the chakras but actively connects you to their specific functions and elemental powers. The combined sensory input and intentional breathing serve to cleanse, balance, and ultimately empower each chakra, allowing the entire energy system to function as a coherent, potent, and integrated whole.

Without mastery of these, one should not rush for higher spiritual operations.

## Level 1: Passive Observation

1.  Sit comfortably with a straight spine. Close your eyes.
2.  Direct your entire, unwavering attention to the point at the very base of your spine, the perineum.
3.  Do nothing else. Do not visualize, do not judge, do not analyze. Simply hold your focus in that specific location. If your mind wanders, gently but firmly return it to the base chakra. Be still and present.
    
    Duration: 5 minutes.
    

## Level 2: Observing the Chakra Vortex

1.  Maintain your focus at the base of the spine.
2.  Begin to visualize the chakra as a sphere of bright, vivid red energy.
3.  See this sphere as a living vortex, spinning clockwise. Do not force it; simply observe it turning, alive and potent. Hold this image with steady, unwavering attention.
    
    Duration: 5 minutes.
    

## Level 3: Chakra Charging

1.  Maintain your focus on the spinning, red vortex at the base of your spine.
2.  As you inhale, visualize the chakra drawing in pure, red energy from the earth around you.
3.  See this energy combine with the vortex, causing it to spin faster and grow brighter and more substantial with each inhalation. Your focus is on the continuous process of absorption and growth.
    
    Duration: 5 minutes.
    

## Level 4: The Synthesis

Maintain your focus at the base of the spine and the visualization of the spinning, red vortex from Level 3.

Engage All Senses: Combine the visualization with a complete sensory experience:

-   Feeling: Feel the physical sensation of the chakra, a tangible warmth, a gentle weight, or a low, vibrating hum at the base of your spine. Feel the energy you inhale as a cool or warm current entering the vortex.
-   Hearing: Internally hear the sound of the spinning vortex, a low, steady, resonant hum or a deep, rhythmic drumming that corresponds to its energy.
-   Smelling: Associate a specific, grounding scent with the chakra, such as the rich smell of damp earth, cedarwood, or musk. Inhale this aroma with your breath.
-   Visualization: See the red vortex with maximum intensity and clarity, a brilliant, living light that grows with every breath.

**Breathing:** Synchronize your breath with the entire process. Each inhalation actively draws in energy and all sensory data, while each exhalation releases any stagnant energy, strengthening the chakra's spin and glow.

**The Goal:** Your entire consciousness must be immersed in this unified, multi-sensory experience.

You are not just visualizing a chakra; you are feeling it, hearing it, smelling it, and actively fueling it with your breath, all simultaneously.

Hold this complete, synthesized state of being for the duration of your meditation.

Duration: 5 minutes.

**How to know you have mastered this:** Level 4 comes effortlessly. You don’t need to wait all the way to Level 4; Level 4 is adept. After Level 2, you can effectively begin using magick and empowering chakras more efficiently.

Perfection is a goal, not the reality of everything. It comes with time. An adept would be able to do Level 4 very easily and maintain it easily for over 3 minutes, without any effort.
