# Ethics Meditation:  
Focusing On Context

author: **High Priest Zevios Metathronos**

This practice systematically deconstructs the barrier between mere reading and true understanding, thereby opening your higher awareness. By moving beyond the literal text to perceive the abstract context that surrounds it, you learn to apprehend the unwritten Law of the Gods, the foundational principles that govern reality.

Merging with a single line through repetition transforms it from a statement into a living current of divine logic, channeling its essence directly into your mind. This process cultivates a deified mode of thought, allowing you to comprehend not just the letter, but the living spirit of divine law.

This is the essential preparation, the attuning of your own consciousness to the divine frequency, which makes any subsequent communication with the Gods not only possible but intelligible.

## Level 1: The Union

1.  Select an Ethic of the Temple of Zeus after you have mastered and know the 36 Ethics very well.
2.  Read it once to understand its logical meaning.
3.  Read it again, this time engaging your heart and emotion, allowing the words to resonate within you.
4.  **Continue reading, holding the text in a state of union where logic, emotion, and intuitive understanding merge into a single, cohesive experience of the text.**
5.  **Do nothing after this, just maintain that state.**

## Level 2: Comprehending Context

1.  After reading the text, set it aside.
2.  **Shift your focus entirely from the words themselves to what surrounds them, the silent, abstract context in which the text exists.**
3.  Meditate on this unseen framework. Feel the space, the principles, and the field of potential that gives the text its meaning. Your entire awareness is held on this abstract, non-conceptual foundation. Simply meditate on this, and nothing else.

## Level 3: Specified Meditation

1.  Select a single, potent line from the text, such as, "We are where we are because of freedom," from Satya’s Ethics, or any other.
2.  **Hold this single line in your mind as a continuous, silent thought. Repeat it in your mind and let creative imagination take over.**
3.  **Do not analyze it. Maintain it with heart, emotion, and logic, and then simply observe its effects on your consciousness.**
4.  Your role is to be a passive receptor, allowing the quote to resonate and channel understanding to you. Meditate on the line itself and the subtle shifts it creates within you.
