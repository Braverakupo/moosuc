Merely turn your focus to the legs and feet for 5 minutes. Allow the energy to flow into them and get a very good feeling for it. Your attention accumulates in the feet chakras and prepares them for opening.

**The above is the awareness level of this meditation.**

### Level 2

Do the same as in Level 1, but also inhale power into all the related chakras shown in the diagram, drawing in **white-gold light mixed with red undertones.** Maintain strong, concentrated light in all the chakras: the thighs, knees, feet, toes, everywhere.

**The above is the empowerment + awareness level of the meditation.**

### Level 3

**Mantra to use:** **HELIOS**

**Number of vibrations:** 10 on the thighs, 10 on the knees, 10 on the feet and the rest of the legs.

**State:** Empowered + Established Awareness
