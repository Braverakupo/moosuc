## Understanding Your Chakras

Think of the chakras as spinning wheels of energy along your spine. Each one vibrates at a specific frequency (corresponding to a color of the visible light spectrum) and governs specific physical, emotional, and spiritual functions.

**The 7 major chakras** run from the base of the spine (red) to the crown of the head (violet). The 6 minor chakras sit at the hips, shoulders, and temples. Together, these 13 energy centers form the complete architecture of the soul.

**Start here:** Learn to feel each chakra before trying to open or empower them. Sit quietly, focus on one chakra at a time, and simply observe what you feel. Warmth, tingling, pulsing, or pressure are all normal signs of awareness developing. Do not force anything.

**The granthis (knots):** Three major energy blocks exist at the sacral, throat, and crown chakras. These must be opened gradually through consistent meditation. Forcing them can produce discomfort. Patience and daily practice are the keys.
