author: **High Priest Zevios Metathronos**

Before opening the Chakras through the Power Meditation section, you must ensure that you have mastered all the previous levels here. When you have done that, the opening of all the chakras can and should take place.

-   [Opening the Soul](OpeningChakras.php)
