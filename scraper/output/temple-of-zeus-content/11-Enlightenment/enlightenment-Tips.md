# Spiritual Warfare Tips

## Spiritual Practice

-   Consistency is everything. A short daily ritual done with focus is worth more than an elaborate working done once a month. Build your spiritual power steadily. The cumulative effect is what breaks through.
-   Always clean your aura and reinforce your aura of protection before and after any offensive spiritual work. This is not optional.
-   Work during planetary hours that correspond to your intention. Mars hours for aggressive workings. Saturn hours for binding. Sun hours for empowerment. This multiplies the effect.
-   Group workings are exponentially more powerful than solitary ones. Coordinate with other Zevists when possible. The Temple of Zeus forums and organized rituals at [tozrituals.org](https://tozrituals.org) are designed for this purpose.
-   Keep a journal of your workings. Record the date, planetary hour, what you did, and any results that follow. Over time, patterns will emerge that show you where your strengths lie.

## Information Warfare

-   Focus your efforts where they have the most impact. Large forums, popular social media discussions, and well-trafficked online spaces are where you reach the most people. Quality over quantity.
-   When debating, stay calm and professional. Let your opponent become emotional while you present facts. Ask questions that expose contradictions. The audience (mostly silent observers) will draw their own conclusions.
-   Always have your references ready. Historical facts, archaeological evidence, and primary sources carry far more weight than opinions. The ancient world left abundant evidence of the original Gods and their worship.
-   Remember: most people in online forums are lurkers who read without posting. The vocal opposition is usually a small minority. Your arguments are being absorbed by many more people than you realize.
-   Correct misinformation about Paganism and Zevism wherever you encounter it. Many people genuinely do not know that the original religions had nothing to do with blood sacrifice, devil worship, or any of the other slanders manufactured by Christianity.

## Personal Conduct

-   Never compromise your safety. Work within the law at all times. Spiritual warfare is conducted through the powers of the mind and soul, not through illegal activity.
-   In countries with blasphemy laws or religious extremism, exercise extreme caution. Your safety comes first. You can do powerful spiritual work in complete privacy and silence.
-   If you encounter religious imposition in public or professional spaces, handle it through proper channels: complaints to management, written correspondence, and (when appropriate) legal avenues. Be professional. Be persistent. Be documented.
-   Do not reveal your spiritual practice to people who would use it against you. The Gods understand discretion. What matters is what is in your heart and what you do in your spiritual practice, not what others see.
