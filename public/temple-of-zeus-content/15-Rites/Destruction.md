# The Destruction Ritual

## Condemnation, Attack, and Retribution

Zeus does not tolerate injustice. The Gods have given us teeth as well as tongues. The Destruction Ritual is the weapon of the Zevist: a formal act of spiritual warfare against an enemy who has caused genuine harm. Through this ritual, the practitioner condemns the target before the Gods, invokes Nemesis (the daughter of Zeus, the Goddess of Divine Retribution) to execute judgement, and directs the full force of their empowered soul against the one who has wronged them.

This is not a request. It is an attack. It is not forgiveness. It is justice delivered by the hand of the practitioner with the backing of the Gods. Zeus gave humanity the power to fight, and Nemesis exists for one purpose: to bring ruin upon those who deserve it.

## On the Right to Destroy

The Abrahamic religions teach submission: "turn the other cheek," "forgive those who trespass against you," "vengeance is mine, saith the Lord." This is the doctrine of slaves. Zevism teaches the opposite: you are not a slave. You are a child of Zeus. When someone strikes you, you strike back. When someone wrongs you, you bring the weight of the Gods upon their head. This is not cruelty. This is the Law of Ma'at: the scales must balance, and if the universe does not balance them on its own, you have the right and the power to balance them yourself.

Nemesis (Νέμεσις), the daughter of Zeus, is the Goddess who ensures that no crime goes unpunished and no arrogance goes unchecked. Her name means "to give what is due." She is not evil. She is the correction. She is the force that descends upon those who have overstepped, who have harmed the innocent, who have abused their power, who believe they can act without consequence. When you invoke Nemesis, you are invoking the justice of Zeus Himself, channeled through His most fearsome daughter.

⚠ MANDATORY WARNING: READ BEFORE PROCEEDING ⚠

**The Destruction Ritual is not to be performed idly, casually, or for trivial reasons.**

This ritual exists for one purpose: the delivery of justice when all other options have been exhausted. It is not for petty grudges. It is not for jealousy. It is not for people who have merely annoyed you, insulted you, or disagreed with you. It is not for ex-lovers who moved on. It is not for rivals you envy. It is not for people you simply dislike.

**Valid reasons for the Destruction Ritual include:**

-   Deliberate, sustained harm committed against you or your family
-   Theft, fraud, or material destruction of your livelihood
-   Betrayal of trust that caused serious damage to your life
-   Deliberate spiritual attack against you
-   Abuse of power by someone in a position of authority over you
-   Slander or defamation that has caused real, measurable damage
-   Physical violence or the credible threat of it

**Invalid reasons include:**

-   Disliking someone
-   Jealousy or envy
-   A disagreement or argument
-   Romantic rejection
-   Petty workplace conflicts
-   Someone having a different opinion than you
-   Anger that will pass in a week

**Nemesis serves Zeus. She serves Ma'at. She serves justice.** If you invoke her against someone who does not deserve it, she will not strike them. She will turn and strike *you*. The scales of Ma'at balance in all directions. A false accusation before the Gods is itself an offense, and it will be treated as one. The force you send out will return upon your own head with the same intensity you directed at the innocent.

Ask yourself before you begin: *If Zeus Himself were to review the evidence, would He rule in my favor?* If the answer is yes, proceed with full conviction and without mercy. If the answer is uncertain, do not proceed. Wait. Reflect. Seek counsel. The ritual will still be available when you are certain.

**But when all other options have been exhausted, when justice has been denied, when the offender believes they are beyond consequence: destroy them without mercy. This is what the ritual is for. This is what Nemesis is for. This is the right that Zeus has given His children.**

## Timing

The Destruction Ritual is most effective during a waning Moon (from full to new phase). For severe cases, perform the ritual every night of the waning phase. Persistence and repetition amplify the force. The darker the Moon, the more destructive the energy available.

Saturday (the day of Saturn/Kronos) and Tuesday (the day of Ares) are the most powerful days for destruction workings.

## What is Required

-   A black candle (representing the destruction of the target's defences)
-   Incense (myrrh or dragon's blood preferred for aggressive workings)
-   A piece of paper with the target's name written on it
-   A fireproof bowl
-   Your focused hatred, your righteous anger, and your absolute conviction that the target deserves what is coming

## The Rite

### Step 1: Opening

Light the black candle and the incense. Begin with the [Standard Ritual](Ritual.php) through Step 3 (the Invocation to Zeus). After the Invocation, do not proceed with the standard prayer. Instead, shift into the Destruction Rite.

### Step 2: The Condemnation

Hold the paper with the target's name. Read aloud the formal condemnation. Be specific. Name the person. Name the crime. Do not generalize. The Gods require precision. This is not a complaint. It is an indictment.

> Zeus, King of the Gods, Lord of Justice, I bring before You a matter of blood.
> 
> The one named **\[full name of target\]** has committed the following against me: *\[state every specific act of harm, betrayal, or injustice in detail\]*.
> 
> This was done deliberately. This was done unjustly. This person believed they could act against me without consequence. They were wrong.
> 
> I condemn **\[name\]** before Zeus. I condemn **\[name\]** before the Gods. I condemn **\[name\]** before Ma'at, whose scales do not lie and whose judgement does not sleep.
> 
> I do not forgive. I do not forget. I demand retribution.

### Step 3: The Invocation of Nemesis

Now invoke Nemesis directly. She is the executioner. You are the prosecutor. Zeus is the judge.

> **NEMESIS!**
> 
> Daughter of Zeus! Goddess of Divine Retribution! She who gives what is due! She from whom no guilty soul escapes!
> 
> I call upon You. I invoke You. I summon Your presence into this ritual and into the life of **\[name\]**.
> 
> Nemesis, hear the crimes that have been committed against me. Hear the condemnation I have spoken before Your Father. See the scales of Ma'at, and see how they are unbalanced.
> 
> I ask You to descend upon **\[name\]**.
> 
> Shatter their confidence.  
> Ruin their plans.  
> Turn their allies against them.  
> Let every lie they have told be exposed.  
> Let every scheme they have built collapse.  
> Let every door they approach be shut in their face.  
> Let sleep flee from them.  
> Let peace abandon them.  
> Let the full weight of what they have done fall upon their own head, compounded, multiplied, and inescapable.
> 
> Nemesis, You who punished Narcissus for his arrogance, You who brought low the proud and the cruel since the beginning of the world: bring low this one. Give them what they are owed. Give them everything they are owed.
> 
> I do not ask for mercy on their behalf. I ask for justice. And if justice is terrible, then let it be terrible.
> 
> In the Name of Zeus, who is Just.  
> In the Name of Nemesis, who is Retribution.  
> Under the Law of Ma'at, whose scales balance in all directions.
> 
> The condemnation is spoken. The invocation is made. The force is released.

### Step 4: The Attack

This is the moment of directed force. Hold the paper with the target's name. Focus every ounce of your will, your anger, and your conviction upon the target. Visualize them clearly. See their face. See them suffering the consequences of what they have done. Do not hold back. Do not dilute the visualization with doubt or guilt. If they deserved mercy, you would not be performing this ritual. They do not deserve mercy. They deserve Nemesis.

Visualize a dark, heavy energy descending upon the target like a storm. See their aura turning grey, then black. See their defences crumbling. See their life falling apart in the specific ways you have demanded. Hold this visualization with absolute intensity for as long as you can sustain it.

When the visualization reaches its peak, light the paper in the flame of the black candle and place it in the fireproof bowl. As it burns, speak:

> As this paper burns, so burns the peace of **\[name\]**.  
> As this flame consumes, so is **\[name\]** consumed by the consequences of their actions.  
> The energy is released. The force is sent. It is irreversible, non-recallable.  
> It is done.

### Step 5: Closing and Cleansing

After the paper is fully burned, sit in silence. Let the energy settle.

Then perform a cleansing: visualize brilliant white light filling your entire body, burning away every trace of the dark energy you directed at the target. You are the weapon, not the victim. The energy you used was directed outward; make sure none of it lingers in your own aura. Vibrate **"SURYA"** (*SOOR-YAH*) nine times to purify yourself with solar fire.

Close with:

> **Praised be Zeus, the God of Gods, the Unconquered and Greatest, Father of Gods and Men, King of the Immortals!**

Extinguish the black candle. The ritual is concluded.

## Repetition

One working is rarely enough. For serious matters, repeat the ritual every night during the waning Moon. Each repetition compounds the force. Each repetition drives the energy deeper into the target's life. Do not stop until you are satisfied that justice has been served, or until the signs of Nemesis's work become visible in the target's circumstances.

## The Law of Ma'at

The Destruction Ritual is real. It produces real effects. It is not a game and it is not therapy for anger. It is a weapon, and like all weapons, it must be used justly. Nemesis is the daughter of Zeus, and she serves justice, not malice. If you use this ritual against someone who has genuinely wronged you, the Gods will back your working and the force will land. If you use it against the innocent, out of petty jealousy, or for trivial reasons, the scales of Ma'at will balance in the other direction, and the force will return upon you.

Be certain of your cause before you begin. Once the invocation of Nemesis is spoken and the paper is burned, there is no taking it back.
