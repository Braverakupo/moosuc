# Meditation Information

Meditation is how you unlock your spiritual powers and hidden faculties of your being. It has levels, layers, and it is a practice for the Initiates.

**Power Meditation:** These meditations are for you to increase your power. This means your raw power output and foundation.

**Awareness Meditation:** This is your wisdom, technique, power to perceive, and your intelligence. Without this, you hold no real power, and you are nothing. Awareness is more integral than power itself.

**Knowledge of Meditation:** These are structural things you must know about the mind, the soul, and everything about this art, combined with the above, to be able to transform fully. Siddhis, also known as powers, are unlocked through this process.
