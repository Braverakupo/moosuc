II. The Zevist Calendar & Sabbath

The eight sacred festivals of the Zevistic year, based on the astrological and calendar cycle.

  1. THE ZEVIST CALENDAR & SABBATH
    • Winter Solstice / Yule (Dec 22-23) - Zeus, Dionysos
    • Imbolc / Candlemas (Early February) - Estia, Artemis
    • Spring Equinox (Mar 21-23) - Astarte
    • Eve of Beltane (April 30) - Aphrodite
    • Summer Solstice (Jun 23-24) - Apollo
    • Lammas / Lughnasadh (August 1) - Demeter
    • Autumn Equinox (Sept 21-23) - Demeter, Persephone
    • Samhain / Hallowmas (October 31) - Persephone, Pluton, Anubis
