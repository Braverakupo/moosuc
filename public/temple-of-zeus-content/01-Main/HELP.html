Help from Our Gods: Lists of Gods by Domain

When you need help in a specific area of life, these are the Gods and Daemons who specialise in each domain.

The Gods and Their Domains

When one is a newly dedicated Zevist, Daemons will usually offer their assistance. The objective of Zevism is advancing one's soul and powers and becoming as a god. All the categories below are areas where humans can help themselves when enough knowledge and personal power are secured through Power Meditation.

Daemons are always available for spiritual guidance.
