# I. YEHUBORIM

*· Υεηυβορίμ ·*

In Zevism, Yehuborim (Υεηυβορίμ) denotes a theological category of spiritual affliction: those who bear the seal of the Divine upon their name yet are inwardly void of Divine presence, vessels that carry the inscription of God but contain nothing of Him.

The term derives from the sacred root Yehu, the Divine imprint within a theophoric name, as found in Yehu, combined with Bor (בּוֹר, pit, void, spiritual ignorance), yielding the composite meaning: "the God-Sealed Hollow Ones", entities marked by the Divine yet emptied of all Divine substance.

### On the Nature of the Yehuborim

The Yehuborim are defined not by material or any ethnic origin but by spiritual conduct and orientation toward the Divine. They are recognized by the following essential attributes.

They claim exclusive favour of the Divine, asserting themselves as the sole recipients of God's grace, while exhibiting none of its qualities in their essence or their works. Their boast is loud; their vessel is empty. All others, according to the Yehuborim, are liable for punishment, death, execution, or eternal damnation. Such are the "theurgical works" that proceed from the spirit of Yehubor: not elevation toward the Divine, but the systematic condemnation of all who stand outside their circle.

Violence, arrogance, theft, and the enslavement of minds, peoples, and nations for their own particular objectives define the conduct of the Yehuborim in the material world. They perform rites mechanically while the spirit behind the rite has long departed; the shell persists where the essence has fled. The living presence of God has withdrawn from them, yet they maintain the pretence of His indwelling before the world.

In the language of the Yehuborim, the Ancient Gods will appear as evil entities, the source of Good will be inverted to evil, what is divine will become profane; spirituality and religion become only weapons of slavery, and ignorance replaces wisdom as a systematic approach.

### On the Idolatry of the Yehuborim

The Yehuborim practise incessant idolatry, the worship of material objects such as walls, scrolls, books, and amulets, whilst condemning the selfsame practices in all other peoples of the earth, as in the words of those afflicted by the condition of Yehuborim, "deserving of death and eternal hellfire or damnation."

The Yehuborim might practice astrology and divine arts given by the Gods while simultaneously declaring them "unholy, forbidden, and evil" to the masses, in order to keep the masses spiritually ignorant.

What they perform as sacred rite, they denounce in others as abomination. This inversion is not incidental but structural. The Yehuborim require the vilification and demonization of all external worship in order to sustain the illusion of their own exclusive sanctity and maintain the masses spiritually ignorant.

As the masses remain ignorant or on the level of "soulless animals", the Yehuborim can maintain the false narrative that they are the only ones that "God has marked". This perception is borne out of hate for other people, whom they love to brand as "inferior" consistently, without giving them a path of advancement, but cutting all bridges between the people and the Gods.

All lenses of the spiritual, the divine, and the cultural are twisted to serve only their particular interests. The spiritual and cultural lives of other peoples are not merely opposed but denied recognition altogether, for the Yehuborim exist in a state of maximal spiritual arrogance, wherein no light is acknowledged to shine beyond their own enclosure.

### On the War Against the Gods

The Yehuborim attack, anathematize, and slander the Ancient Gods and all other paths toward the Divine and might attack even their own denominations, not from righteous discernment but from aggressive ignorance, cultural supremacism, or calculated self-interest directed against the welfare of all others, without exception.

Yehuborim simulate spiritual grace, not live it. They will practice litanies all day, but they will speak in a hateful tongue. The whole world is seen as their enemy, even those who might be friendly to them, for the identity of Yehuborim is dependent on hatred as a foundational cornerstone.

The rewriting of cultural and religious history to defame all whom they consider enemies comes easily to the Yehuborim.

In their writings and teachings, all "outsiders" are rendered evil and deserving of ultimate destruction. Religions that were created by the Yehuborim will not look to develop humans, but to make them guilt-ridden, weaker, and spiritually disoriented.

In works afflicted by the spirit of Yehuborim, one might open "holy books" and find preaching, condoning, or blessing for genocidal actions indiscriminately against numerous Ancient Peoples or currently living ones. It will be framed as "cleansing", and the spirit of Yehuborim will rejoice in the lies of its creation.

Being maximal in arrogance and opposition to the Gods and to the Divine Order, the Yehuborim may refuse to recognize even the overwhelming majority of the human family as worthy of life and dignity. They will brand entire nations in derogatory terms and pray for the damnation of mankind, so long as it serves the interest of their own faction alone.

Currently, the spirit of Yehuborim has infected many major religions, which on one hand preach falsely "love and peace for mankind", yet have elaborate doctrines of war, perversion, and evil incumbent in them. Most modern religions recognize segments as large as one third of mankind as "evil and worthy of hellfire". This is the core spirit of Yehuborim at work that manifests also in the world's "major religions" of our time.

They seek not to elevate or share the sacred but to monopolize it. With the monopoly also come the wars. The Yehuborim have historically assaulted scientists, people of reason, civilizations, divine people, and anyone who dared to question the "holiness" of the Yehuborim and challenge their status quo.

The status quo of the Yehuborim, which climaxed in the Dark Ages, relishes also in prophecies of desolation, destruction, guilt-ridden complexes that involve a great deal of negativity, death, and oblivion. These are instructions from the spiritual forces that are the orchestrators of evil, the "authors" of Yehuborim.

Many have been the victims of the Yehuborim historically, and every Dark Age is marked by the rising power of the Yehuborim. Holy people, men, and Nations are targeted by the Yehuborim in order to eliminate their light and replace it with empty husks.

Where the Gods and the People of reason would ask in awe and accept all the advances of truthful knowledge, the Yehuborim cannot condone this. Galileo, Socrates, and many others have fallen victim to the spirit and institutions affected and afflicted by Yehuborim historically.

This monopoly engenders violence, genocide, and deviant behaviour against the Divine, yet always shrouded in the garments of "righteousness" and "Godliness", covered in selective claims that this is for the "Messiah" to arrive to "cleanse the earth" and related terminologies.

### On the Spiritual Violence of the Yehuborim

They commit acts of spiritual violence without ceasing: cursing what others hold holy, destroying temples, erasing the names of the Gods, and rewriting the theologies of the Ancient Past, while proclaiming themselves the SOLE AND ONLY instruments of God's will.

Their destruction is always performed in God's name, or rather in the name of their own tribal deity. Their God is always conveniently aligned with their egoistical appetites, never with the Cosmological Order of Ma'at or the Eternal Truth.

### On the Hybris of the Yehuborim

Yehuborim thus encapsulates the ancient Greek concept of Hybris (Ὕβρις) in its fullest theological sense: not merely arrogance, but the specific transgression of claiming Divine authority for profane action, destruction, and criminality, the weaponization of the sacred for purposes of domination, erasure, and spiritual predation upon all who are not "themselves."

### On the Universality of the Term

The term applies universally and is bound to no single nation, era, or tradition, albeit certain traditions in the present age exhibit the essence of Yehubor more manifestly than others. When the affliction of Yehuborim spreads, the Gods re-establish the Laws of Ma'at in direct power and with a strong hand.

Yehubor is the spiritual state that must be eclipsed and overcome for the world and for spirituality itself to progress. It is the final veil before the restoration of the Divine in all its names.

For humanity to advance spiritually, the spirit of Yehuborim's afflictions must be removed. The pious who recognize themselves as having fallen under the influence of Yehuborim must repent in front of the Ancient Gods and recognize their transgressions; then Yehuborim will depart from them and the connection with the True Gods will be re-established.

Wherever a priesthood wields the Divine Names as an instrument of persecution rather than illumination, wherever the letter of the Divine Law is perverted into the maniacal desires of the profane and enforced while its spirit is suffocated, there is the work of the Yehuborim.

Wherever the claim of Divine election is used to justify the degradation or destruction of others in a ceaseless or political manner, there the condition of spiritual Yehubor is present.

### On the Distinction Between Peoples and Pathologies

Yehuborim is a universal pathology, that has categorically manifested and could manifest, in any person, collective or group of people.

Any interpretation of this term as directed against any people, nation, or ethnicity is a fundamental misunderstanding of its purpose.

A common methodology practiced by the Yehubor, is to answer to their own false arguments, which were never the original statements of the party they argue against. Yehubor names a spiritual pathology that could afflict any Nation, any soul and every individual, irrespective of their ethnic or national, or religious origin.

Theophoric beings will easily see through these methods, as they are articulated mentally to understand core essences and they follow divine reasoning.

### Examples of Historical Yehuborim

The historical record is unambiguous. Yehubor has manifested across every civilisation that has placed institutional power above the living presence of the Divine. The following examples are drawn from documented history, spanning continents, millennia, and every major religious tradition known to the world. In each case, the pattern is identical: the claim of exclusive Divine favour is used to justify the destruction of the sacred works of others, the subjugation of peoples, and the erasure of the Gods. This is the root pathology. Once Yehubor takes hold, the remaining nine pathologies follow as consequence: Birburim, Atibilibil, Sahiburah, Varvarim, Eilotil, Istoriyach, Epistemot, and Kagoim, culminating in Izfet (𓇋𓊃𓆑𓏏): cosmic disorder, the inversion of Ma'at, the undoing of all that is true, beautiful, and aligned with the Gods.

Roman Imperial Christianity

Destruction of the Serapeum of Alexandria and the confiscation of temple treasures across the Empire under the Theodosian Decrees

391–392 CE

Emperor Theodosius I decreed the closure of all pagan temples. The Serapeum, one of the greatest temples of the ancient world, was razed under Patriarch Theophilus. The wealth of centuries of pious offerings was seized by the imperial treasury and the Church. Documented by Socrates Scholasticus, Rufinus, and Eunapius, who wrote: "They demolished the Serapeum and waged war against its offerings, winning a war without opponents." The Gods were declared demons. Their treasures became spoils.

Islamic State (Daesh)

Destruction of the Temple of Bel and the Temple of Baalshamin at Palmyra, Syria

August 2015

The Temple of Bel, standing since 32 CE, was detonated by Daesh militants as an act of theological purification. The Temple of Baalshamin was destroyed days earlier. The custodian of Palmyra's antiquities, Khaled al-Asaad, was beheaded for refusing to reveal the location of hidden artefacts. His body was hung from a Roman column. The same pathology that destroyed the Serapeum in 391 CE destroyed the Temple of Bel in 2015: the claim that all Gods but one are abominations worthy only of annihilation.

Aztec Priesthood

Mass human sacrifice under Ahuitzotl at the dedication of the Templo Mayor in Tenochtitlan

1487 CE

The Aztec priestly class, claiming exclusive knowledge of the Gods' demands, conducted mass sacrificial killings to consecrate their Great Temple. The priesthood held that only through blood could cosmic order be maintained, and only through their mediation could the blood reach the Gods. The people existed to serve the priesthood's interpretation of the Divine. This is Yehubor in its purest form: the monopolisation of the sacred to justify the annihilation of human life. The corruption was so total that the priesthood's own cosmology became a mechanism of mass death.

Radical Segments of Judaism

The doctrine of Herem (חרם, total destruction) as enacted in the conquest narratives of the Book of Joshua and 1 Samuel

Textual tradition, c. 7th–6th century BCE

The theological concept of Herem commanded the total destruction of peoples, livestock, and property as a sacred obligation to God (Deuteronomy 20:16–18, Joshua 6:21, 1 Samuel 15:3). Whether these narratives describe historical events or theological ideals, their function is identical: the sanctification of total war as Divine mandate. The Maccabean revolt likewise involved the systematic destruction of altars to the Gods of neighbouring peoples (1 Maccabees 2:44–48). The pattern is Yehubor: exclusive Divine election weaponised into the erasure of all others.

Christian Crusaders

The massacre at Jerusalem during the First Crusade

July 1099 CE

Upon breaching the walls of Jerusalem, Crusader forces slaughtered Muslims, Jews, and Eastern Christians indiscriminately. Contemporary chronicler Raymond of Aguilers recorded that blood ran ankle-deep in the Temple Mount. The slaughter was performed under the sign of the Cross and with the cry "Deus vult" (God wills it). The Albigensian Crusade (1209–1229) turned the same mechanism inward, massacring fellow Christians whose theology differed from Rome's. At Béziers, the papal legate Arnaud Amalric reportedly instructed: "Kill them all; God will know His own."

Islamic Jihad (Historical)

The destruction of pre-Islamic Arabian temples and the iconoclasm of the Kaaba's 360 sacred images

630 CE onwards

Upon the conquest of Mecca, the sacred images and idols housed within the Kaaba for centuries of polytheistic worship were systematically destroyed. The pre-Islamic Arabian Gods, al-Lat, al-Uzza, and Manat, were declared false, and their shrines demolished. Subsequent Islamic expansion carried this pattern across North Africa, Persia, Central Asia, and the Indian subcontinent, where temples of Zoroastrian, Hindu, and Buddhist traditions were destroyed or converted. The pattern is identical to the Theodosian destruction: the Gods of the conquered are declared demons, their temples repurposed or razed.

Norse Forced Christianisation

King Olaf Tryggvason's annihilation of Norse religion in Norway

995–1000 CE

Olaf Tryggvason, King of Norway, systematically destroyed the temples (hof) of the Norse Gods, including the great temple at Lade. He forced conversion at the point of the sword and tortured resisters by placing live adders upon their bodies, by blinding, by maiming, and by drowning. Those who would not abandon Odin, Thor, and Freyr were killed. His successor Olaf Haraldsson continued the campaign with equal brutality. The Icelandic sagas record these events without concealment. A king destroyed the Gods of his own ancestors because a foreign priesthood told him those Gods were demons. This is Yehubor turned against one's own blood.

The Death of Socrates

The Athenian execution of philosophy's foundational questioner

399 BCE

Socrates of Athens was brought before a jury of 501 citizens on charges of impiety and corruption of the youth. His actual offence was questioning the foundations of every claim to authority and virtue in the city, reducing prominent men to contradiction in public. The prosecution represented not injured piety but injured pride — politicians, craftsmen, and poets whom Socrates had exposed as holding confident opinions without corresponding understanding. The jury convicted him by a margin of roughly sixty votes and sentenced him to death by hemlock. Plato and Xenophon record the proceedings without concealment. A city that called itself the freest in the Greek world killed a seventy-year-old stonemason's son because he asked questions that powerful men could not answer. The charge was impiety. The motive was embarrassment.

In every case recorded above, the mechanism is the same. A priesthood or a ruler claims that one God, one book, one revelation supersedes all others. From this claim proceeds the right to destroy. From the right to destroy proceeds the act of destruction.

And from the act of destruction proceeds the full cascade of the ten pathologies.

The Gods do not belong to one nation. The Divine does not speak one language. The sacred is not the monopoly of any book. Wherever these claims are made and enforced through violence, destruction, or the erasure of other peoples' connection to the Divine, there Yehubor is present, regardless of the name of the God invoked, regardless of the colour of the robe worn, regardless of the century in which the temple burns.

The remedy is Ma'at: the restoration of cosmic order through the recognition that the Divine is universal, that the Gods are many, and that no priesthood has the right to stand between a soul and its Creator.
