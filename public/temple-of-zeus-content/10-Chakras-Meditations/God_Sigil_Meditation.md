# God Sigil Meditation

author: **High Priest Zevios Metathronos**

The God Sigil meditation is a foundation to become aware of, learn how to connect, and interact with the energies of the Gods through their blueprints/sigils and Names. Without mastering this, communication can become impossible or difficult. When this is mastered, you can proceed later on to greater communication. Master the methods below.

## Level 1: The Observer

1.  Select a sigil. It can be a planetary seal, a personal creation, or a symbol of power.
2.  Place the image before you or hold it clearly in your mind.
3.  For a set period, simply observe the sigil with unwavering, non-judgmental attention. Trace its lines with your eyes. Do not analyze its meaning or history. Your only task is to see the form completely and absorb its geometric structure.
4.  Do nothing but observe the sigil and let it be imprinted in your mind.

## Level 2: Visualization & Perception

1.  Close your eyes or look away from the physical sigil.
2.  Recreate the image of the sigil in your mind’s eye with perfect precision.
3.  Hold this internally generated sigil in a state of unwavering focus. It must remain stable, clear, and undistorted. If it wavers or fades, gently but firmly reform it until it is a solid, immovable object of your concentration.

Difference of Level 2: You must now start meditating logically and emotionally on the sigil. Judge nothing of what happens. Just observe what emotions and logical thoughts come up.

## Level 3: The Animator

1.  While holding the sigil perfectly in your mind, begin to imbue it with life.
2.  Visualize it starting to glow with an inner light. See it begin to spin or pulse with a slow, steady rhythm.
3.  Your task is to transform the static image into a dynamic, energetic vortex of power. Feel its energy and hold this animated, living sigil with complete focus.
4.  Imagine at this stage that you are perceiving the energy of the God Himself.
5.  Judge nothing of this. Proceed without inhalation or anything else. Just observe any and all information that comes up, feelings of power, or related sensations.

## Level 4: The Merger: Invoking the Sigil of the God

Without having sufficiently bonded with a Sigil from above, this might not work. Return back and ensure you have mastered previous steps.

1.  Maintain the animated, glowing sigil in your mind.
2.  Vibrate a Divine Name of the God as you hold the sigil in your mind or as you see it in front of you. Living imagination of the sigil is always preferred.
3.  With each breath, begin to draw the sigil’s energy into yourself. As you inhale, feel its power flowing into the center of your being.
4.  As you exhale, feel your own essence flowing into the sigil, strengthening its form.
5.  Then, the Sigil is alive and flows back into you.
6.  Continue this reciprocal energy exchange until the boundary between you and the sigil dissolves. You and the sigil become one unified field of power and intent. Hold this state of complete merger and resonance. When this is established and stable, you have connected to the invoked energy.
7.  Most importantly, meditate on this very energy after you have established the connection.

**How to know you have mastered this:** Level 4 will become effortless, and all the previous levels very easy. Merging will be quick and direct.
