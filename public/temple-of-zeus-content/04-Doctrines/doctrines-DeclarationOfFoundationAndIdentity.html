What has been removed should never have been there.

All objections derive from the assumption that something other than the Temple holds authority over the Temple's identity. Nothing other than the Temple holds that authority. This Declaration restates the obvious for those who have forgotten it.

#Article VII: Permanence and Sovereignty

This Declaration is permanent. It is not a policy statement subject to revision by future leadership. It is a constitutional principle of the Temple of Zeus, binding upon all present and future office-holders, all present and future members, and all present and future expressions of the institution in any medium, in any language, in any jurisdiction.

The foundation, the sovereignty, the doctrine, and the identity of the Temple of Zeus, as declared in this document, are the institutional facts of this religious body. They are not opinions. They are not negotiable. They are not subject to amendment by external pressure, by encyclopedic edit, by academic re-classification, by legal challenge from parties without standing, or by any other means by which a party outside the constituted authority of the Temple might seek to rewrite the institution's self-description.

The right of an institution to declare its own foundation, to identify its own founder, to name its own doctrine, and to set the terms by which it shall be referenced is anterior to the existence of any party that would dispute that right. The Temple of Zeus exercises this right here, in writing, on the public record, under the seal of its Founder, and with the authority of the Ancient Gods to whom the institution answers.

The eternal cannot be subordinated to the temporal. The Gods are eternal. The Temple is the institution by which the eternal Gods are honoured in the present age. The categories, classifications, and characterisations of the temporal age cannot bind the eternal, and they shall not bind the Temple that serves the eternal. Encyclopedias, academic compendia, journalistic profiles, and legal challenges all belong to the temporal order. They are not the measure of religious truth. They are the documents of the present moment, and the present moment is shorter than the memory of the Gods.

Should any future leader of the Temple attempt to reverse the foundational principles declared here, that act shall constitute, by the terms of this Declaration, a violation of the Temple's constitutional principles, a betrayal of the trust of the community, and an offence against the Gods who govern the Temple's existence. The community and the wider spiritual lineage are hereby authorised, by this Declaration, to resist any such attempt and to treat it as the institutional danger it would represent.

This Declaration is final. It is not a draft. It is not a request for comment. It is the institutional self-declaration of a sovereign religious body, issued under the authority of its Founder, signed, dated, and made permanent. It stands until the cosmos that gave rise to the Gods who govern this institution stands no longer. That is to say, it stands forever.

The Temple of Zeus declares itself. The declaration stands. It does not require permission, and it shall not be revised.
